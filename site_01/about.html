Our Story

Shell Surfing School was born in 2010 from a deep love for the ocean and a passion for sharing the thrill of surfing with the world. Founded by a group of seasoned surfers and ocean enthusiasts, our mission has always been to make surfing accessible, safe, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of age or skill level.

Humble Beginnings

It all started on the sunny beaches of California, where our founders, Jake Thompson and Sarah Miller, spent their days riding waves and teaching friends how to surf. What began as informal lessons on the beach quickly grew into a small but dedicated community of surfers. Word spread about their unique teaching methods, which combined technical expertise with a fun, laid-back approach. Soon, they realized they could turn their passion into a business that would inspire others to connect with the ocean.

Building a Legacy

In 2012, Shell Surfing School officially opened its doors in Malibu, California. With just a handful of instructors and a few surfboards, we welcomed our first students. Our early days were filled with challenges, but our commitment to quality and safety helped us grow. By 2015, we had expanded to multiple locations along the West Coast, including San Diego and Santa Cruz.

Innovation and Growth

As the demand for surfing lessons grew, so did our vision. We introduced innovative programs like personalized coaching, group sessions, and even surf camps for kids. Our team of certified instructors grew, each bringing their unique expertise and love for the sport. We also partnered with top surfboard and wetsuit brands to provide our students with the best equipment available. In 2018, we launched our online platform, offering virtual lessons and surf tutorials. This allowed us to reach aspiring surfers worldwide, from the shores of Australia to the beaches of Europe. Our commitment to sustainability also became a core part of our mission. We started organizing beach cleanups and partnered with environmental organizations to protect the oceans we love.

A Global Community

Today, Shell Surfing School is more than just a business—it’s a global community of surfers united by a shared passion for the ocean. We’ve taught thousands of students, from complete beginners to advanced riders looking to refine their skills. Our alumni include competitive surfers, ocean advocates, and everyday people who simply love the thrill of catching a wave.

Our Philosophy

At Shell Surfing School, we believe surfing is more than just a sport—it’s a way of life. It’s about connecting with nature, challenging yourself, and finding joy in the simple act of riding a wave. Our instructors are not just teachers; they’re mentors who inspire confidence, respect for the ocean, and a sense of adventure.

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future, our goal is to continue spreading the stoke of surfing while staying true to our roots. We’re exploring new locations, developing eco-friendly surf gear, and creating programs that make surfing accessible to underserved communities. Whether you’re a first-timer or a seasoned pro, we invite you to join us on this incredible journey.
